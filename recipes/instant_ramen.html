Instant Ramen

Instant Ramen for the college students

  • Instant Ramen
  • Boiled Egg
  • Chopped Scallions
  • Fried Garlic
  • Chili Oil (optional)
  1. Boil water in a large pot
  2. When water boils, add seasoning pack and noodles
  3. Cook for 5 minutes
  4. Serve with a mix of ingredients and enjoy
