Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Delicious Scrambled Eggs perfect in every breakfast with simple ingredients!

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cooking Oil
  1. Beat eggs in a bowl
  2. Heat cooking pan and add cooking oil
  3. Pour Eggs on cooking pan
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  5. Put lid and cook for 30 seconds on medium heat
  6. Serve and enjoy
